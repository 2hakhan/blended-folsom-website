Meet the Team

Julie Ivanov

With years of experience assisting in intricate brain and spine surgeries, Registered Nurse and licensed esthetician Julie Ivanov brings a uniquely precise, medical approach to aesthetics. Trained by leading plastic surgeons and aesthetic experts, she holds advanced certifications in neurotoxins, dermal fillers, and laser procedures. Julie’s background in surgery allows her to treat each client with exceptional care and attention to detail, prioritizing both safety and artistry.

Now based in Folsom, California, Julie serves a wide range of clients seeking subtle, effective enhancements without the downtime or commitment of surgery. Her approach is deeply personalized—focused on creating natural results that align with each individual’s goals. Whether it’s smoothing fine lines, restoring volume, or refreshing the skin, Julie’s mission is to help clients feel confident and radiant in their everyday lives.

Medical Director

Dr. Seth Eberlin

Dr. Seth Eberlin is a board-certified plastic surgeon with advanced training in cosmetic, reconstructive, hand, and burn surgery. A Yale graduate and USC Keck School of Medicine alum, he completed his plastic surgery residency at USC, training alongside world leaders in aesthetics. Dr. Eberlin specializes in body contouring, breast, and facial procedures, combining a keen eye for detail with cutting-edge techniques.

Known for his collaborative and education-forward approach, Dr. Eberlin works closely with each patient to develop personalized treatment plans. Outside of surgery, he enjoys outdoor adventures with his wife and three daughters and brings the same passion and precision to both his professional and personal life.
